Introduction

Hello, there! My name is Sam Zyra. I'm 26 years old and just moved to Oregon with my wife and our two cats. I graduated in December 2019 with a BA in English, Technical and Professional Writing - then I got married the next day!

Where I'm From

I grew up in Norman, Oklahoma and moved to Bloomington, Indiana at age 14, two weeks before my freshman year of high school. I now live near Portland, Oregon.

What are your favorite hobbies?

In my free time I'm a writer and a gamer. I write some poetry and have had two poem published in Tributaries, a literary magazine done by IU East. I also write sci-fantasy and love world-building. Games-wise, I play a lot of Star War: The Old Republic, Minecraft (and various mods in it), and Destiny 2.

What's your dream job?

I would love to work in the marketing department for a company that I could be proud of, but as far as dreams go? I want to be a screen-writer. I love my stories, or at least the few that I finish, and the worlds I build to write them in, but I've always dreamed of seeing my worlds brought to life on the big screen.

Where do you live?

As stated before, I currently live near Portland Oregon and have for all of a week, as of the day I'm writing this. It's already wowed me with gorgeous trees and mountains, and the climate is infinitely more appealing than the humidity and heat of Bloomington.

Why do you want to be a web developer?

Web development is a massive part of any marketing job, and it's a growing market all on its own. As a human being with a need to eat, I want to learn these skills in order to find and land a career that I can enjoy and still survive by. Coding in general has more appeal to me, however, given its wide range of applications in game development and CGI.
